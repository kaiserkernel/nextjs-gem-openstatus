title: Status Page Subscribers

<Note>This is a paid feature only</Note>

## What does it mean to subscribe to a status page?

Your subscribers will receive an email notification when you update your status page. Each time you create a new status report or update an existing one, your subscribers will receive an email.

As a status page owner you will be able to see the list of subscribers.

## How to subscribe to a status page?

To subscribe to a status page, you need to click on the `Subscribe` button on the status page.

You will be asked to enter your email address. You will receive a confirmation email to validate your subscription.
