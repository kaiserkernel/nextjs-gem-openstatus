---
title: What regions do we support?
order: 4
---

We support one region for each continent to allow multi-regions monitoring:

- America: `iad` (Virginia)
- Europe: `ams` (Amsterdam)
- Asia: `hkg` (Hong-Kong)
- Oceania: `syd` (Sydney)
- Africa: `jnb` (Johannesburg)
- South America: `gru` (Sao Paulo)

Feel free to [contact us](mailto:ping@openstatus.dev) if you need a specific
region.
